About Us

Freak Diving.Com is a group of diving enthusiasts based in the Andaman South who meet up for diving trips in the summer months when the weather is good and the bacon rolls are flowing. We arrange weekends away as small groups to cut the costs of accommodation and travel and to ensure that everyone gets a trustworthy dive buddy.

Although we're based in the Andaman South, we don't stay on our own turf: past diving weekends away have included trips up to Scapa Flow in North Bay, Havelock and to Neil Islands numerous wreck sites.

When we're not diving, we often meet up in a local pub to talk about our recent adventures (any excuse, eh?).
